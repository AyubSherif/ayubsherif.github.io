About Me

I’m Ayub Sherif — a Data Analyst II at Loeb Electric. I bring a unique blend of engineering, physics, and data science to my work.
What sets me apart is my scientific mindset. I don’t just build models or dashboards—I understand the science behind the data. Whether I’m forecasting warehouse utilization using ML, or visualizing data in Power BI, I bring technical intuition and curiosity to every challenge.
I hold a B.S. and M.S. in Nuclear Engineering from The Ohio State University. Currently, I am pursuing a Master’s in Data Science at the University of Colorado Boulder.
I love learning and teaching others. My team often jokingly call it "Ayub University."
I believe in raising the bar—for myself and for the teams I work with. As my supervisor at Loeb Electric once said, “Ayub takes a project and pushes it to 120%. He shows everyone what they didn’t know they needed.”
